What is Pet Axis Avenue?

Our goal at Pet Axis Avenue is to provide all pet owners (whether future, new or seasoned) a single, accessible destination to learn all about their companions!

According to a 2020 study conducted by TrackingHappiness.com, pet owners are 12% happier than those without pets.

  • Find a pet that most suits your personality type!
  • Ensure your surroundings are suitable for your pet before adoption!
  • If you have children, ensure the pet you choose is kid-friendly!
  • Don't adopt a high-maintenance pet if you are busy!

Pet Information Catalogue

Dogs

    Lifespan: 10-13 Years

    Temperament: Golden Retrievers are the friendliest, Shar-peis and Shiba Inus are the least.

    Favorite Food: Beef, Pork, Lamb and Horse meat.

    Kid-Friendly: Most breeds except Chihuahuas, Pekingese and Huskies.

    Stranger-Friendly: Some breeds can be trained to deter strangers.

    Maintenance Level: High

Cats

    Lifespan: 12-18 Years

    Temperament: Depending on the breed, either attention-seeking or attention hating.

    Favorite Food: Pork, Chicken, Beef, Salmon, Tuna and Turkey.

    Kid-Friendly: Most breeds except Russian Blue, Himalayan and Savannah.

    Stranger-Friendly: Yes

    Maintenance Level: Medium-High

Rabbits

    Lifespan: 8-12 Years

    Temperament: Shy, curious, high-spirited.

    Favorite Food: Bell peppers, Bok choy, Brussels sprouts, Carrot tops, Hay and Cucumber.

    Kid-Friendly: Yes

    Stranger-Friendly: Yes

    Maintenance Level: Medium

Birds

    Lifespan: 6-18 Years

    Temperament: Affectionate, possessive, entertaining.

    Favorite Food: Insects, Seeds, grasses, Flowers, Berries, Fruits and Nuts.

    Kid-Friendly: Yes

    Stranger-Friendly: Yes

    Maintenance Level: Medium-Low

Reptiles

    Lifespan: Average 20 Years. The larger it is, the longer it lives.

    Temperament: Neutral, calm. Aggressive only when provoked.

    Favorite Food: Rats, Mice, Rabbits, Smaller reptiles, Insects and Amphibians.

    Kid-Friendly: No

    Stranger-Friendly: Usually not.

    Maintenance Level: Low

Horses

    Lifespan: 25-30 Years

    Temperament: Social, sometimes aloof.

    Favorite Food: Watermelon, Apples, Strawberries, Bananas, Alfalfa and Peppermints.

    Kid-Friendly: Yes

    Stranger-Friendly: Yes

    Maintenance Level: Very High

Hamsters

    Lifespan: 2-5 Years

    Temperament: Docile

    Favorite Food: Grains, Vegetables, Fruits, Timothy hay, Cheese, Rice and Peas.

    Kid-Friendly: Yes

    Stranger-Friendly: Yes

    Maintenance Level: Low

Fish

    Lifespan: 3-5 Years

    Temperament: Friendly, except for Piranhas.

    Favorite Food: Brine shrimp, Krill, Daphnia, Blackworms and Tubifex.

    Kid-Friendly: Yes, except for Piranhas.

    Stranger-Friendly: Yes, except for Piranhas.

    Maintenance Level: Low

Fun Facts

The real name of the internet sensation "Grumpy Cat " was Tardar Sauce.
The Ancient Egyptians did not worship cats. Rather, their gods Bastet, Sekhmet and Mafdet were depicted with cat-like heads. However, if one was to kill a cat, one would be sentenced to death.
Dogs have an excellent sense of smell, and can use it to diagnose medical conditions in humans.
The Beatles song "A Day in the Life " has a frequency that only dogs can hear.
A baby rabbit is called a kit, a female is called a doe, and a male is called a buck.
When happy, rabbits perform a highly athletic leap called a 'binky' which involves twists and kicks.
Snakes smell with their tongues!
Hamsters were "discovered " in the Syrian desert less than 100 years ago.
Horses cannot breathe through their mouth, but they can sleep standing up!
The chicken is the closest living relative to the Tyrannosaurus Rex.
Most fish (except sharks) do not have eyelids.
Dory from Finding Nemo is a Blue Hippo Tang whereas Marlin and Nemo are clownfish.
